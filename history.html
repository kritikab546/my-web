The Beginning of AI

The idea of intelligent machines began in early research on logic, mathematics, and computing. Scientists started imagining whether machines could reason like humans. In the 1950s, this dream turned into reality when researchers officially introduced the concept of Artificial Intelligence and began creating programs that could solve problems, make decisions, and learn like humans.

AI Basics

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a technology that allows machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. This includes things like learning from experience, understanding language, recognizing images, solving problems, and making decisions.Artificial Intelligence is the ability of machines to think, learn, and act like humans. It helps computers understand information, find patterns, and make smart decisions.

Types of AI

Artificial Narrow Intelligence

AI that can do only one specific task very well.

Artificial General Intelligence

AI that can think, learn, understand, and make decisions like a human.

Artificial Superintelligence

AI that is smarter than humans in every field — creativity, logic.

Key Components of AI

Machine Learning

A method where AI learns from data and improves over time without being directly programmed.

Neural Networks

A computer system inspired by the human brain, made of layers that process information and find patterns.

Deep Learning

An advanced form of neural networks with many layers, allowing AI to understand complex tasks.

Natural Processing

A technology that helps AI understand and respond to human language. AI in daily life and industries.

Why AI Is Important

AI can handle tasks faster than humans — like sorting data, analyzing information, or generating results instantly. Example: Quick search suggestions, instant translations.

AI takes over boring, repeated work so humans can focus on bigger tasks. Example: Auto-reply emails, automated customer service.
